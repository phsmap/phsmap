IMPORTANT NOTICES

THIS IS RESTRICTED INFORMATION! Any map or document that provides information about the inside of an MCPS facility is considered sensitive information and cannot be disseminated to or accessible to the general public. This means that should you decide to download, print out or otherwise export the data in this application, you are responsible for keeping it secure and adhering to this policy. The only people allowed to have access to this data are students, staff and authorized visitors (such as students coming in from other schools for athletic events, or substitute teachers).



DO NOT RELY ON THIS INFORMATION DURING AN EMERGENCY! This information may not be up to date or reflect real-world conditions (i.e. broken emergency equipment, blocked exits, etc). You should not use this map as your sole source of information during an emergency. You should always take the time to familiarize yourself with your surroundings before an emergency and follow staff instructions during one.



APP CONTROLS

  • Use one finger to pan the map and two to zoom in/out.

  • To view a given point's metadata (i.e. to check if a certain room is a lab), double click on that point.

  • To show/hide different map layers (i.e. room numbers, major landmarks, stairs, etc.), use the Layers menu.

  • To switch which part of campus you're focusing on, use the Switch Maps menu.

  • To download an image of a map, take a screenshot or right-click anywhere on the map and hit "Save image as" (or other similar button). Please be responsible in storing, using and sharing this data.
