Why is a sign in necessary anyways?

To avoid having to constantly re-enter your logins, don't use incognito/private mode, say Yes when Microsoft asks if you want to stay signed in, and don't reload the map page once you're on it (once you've loaded the map page, you can continue to use it even if your access token expires or you lose internet).
